Gran Canaria

Gran Canaria is an island located on the Atlantic Ocean, west from the coast of Morocco. It is the third largest and most populous of the Canary Islands. It is often referred to as "the miniature continent", due to the variety of climates, landscapes and beaches found. Most of the tourists visit the southern part, while the northern tends to be cooler. The capital city is Las Palmas de Gran Canaria.

Ibiza

Ibiza is a Spanish island located 150 kilometers east from Valencia. It is the third largest of Balearic Islands, an autonomous community of Spain. The island has become to be associated with nightlife, electronic dance music and the club scene, all of which attract the large amount of tourists. An interesting fact about the island is it found its way into the game Test Drive Unlimited 2, in which the player can drive around the entire island.

Florida

Florida is one out of 50 states, located at the south-east coast of United States of America - the home of American vacations. At the hottest state of the US, it is also a home of Disney World, alligators, Daytona International Speedway (the most known NASCAR race track). Here you can enjoy 820 miles (1320 kilometers) of beaches, thousands of palms and fresh water springs. And if you want to watch something spectacular, be sure to visit the Kennedy Space Centre.
